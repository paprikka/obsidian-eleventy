Caused by the damage to [Broca's Area](<../Broca's Area>).

Inability to formulate language. 

- slow, halting speech
- difficulty in forming sentences
- comprehension remains (almost?) intact

Compare with [Wernicke's Aphasia](<../Wernicke's Aphasia>)
